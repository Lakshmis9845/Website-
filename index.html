Courses

Hindustani Classical Music

Learn the traditional art of Hindustani music with our expert instructors. Our journey is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Hindustani Classical Music. Our seasoned instructors bring deep knowledge and passion, guiding students through the intricate nuances and expressive elements of this ancient tradition. From foundational techniques to advanced compositions, our classes are designed to help students master and appreciate the depth of Hindustani Classical Music.

Tabla

Master the rhythms and techniques of tabla with our comprehensive courses.Our experienced instructors are dedicated to sharing their passion and expertise in Tabla, guiding each student on their musical path. We offer beginner to advanced classes, each designed to build your skills through structured lessons and personalized guidance

Harmonium

Develop your skills on the harmonium with personalized lessons. Our story reflects our dedication to teaching the Harmonium and sharing its rich musical tradition with students of all levels. Our skilled instructors bring a wealth of experience and a love for the Harmonium, helping each student develop their unique musical abilities. From foundational techniques to advanced performance skills, our classes are tailored to guide and inspire every learner.

Sugama Sangeetha

Explore the beauty of Sugama Sangeetha,with our experienced faculty. Our story is rooted in our commitment to nurturing the appreciation and practice of Sugama Sangeeth, a genre known for its melodious and lyrical qualities. Our experienced instructors are passionate about teaching Sugama Sangeeth, providing students with the skills to perform and enjoy this beautiful music style. Our classes cater to all levels, offering personalized instruction to help each student explore and excel in Sugama Sangeeth.
